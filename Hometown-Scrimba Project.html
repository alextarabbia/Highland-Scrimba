Visit Highland

Enjoy four beautiful seasons in upstate New York located in between New York City and Albany.

Top three activities to do in Highland

Visit Wilklow's Orchard

Pick some apples, feed goats, or snack on some apple cider donuts.

Eat some local New York grub

Grab some pizza from one of the many tasty pizzarias, have some cider at Bad Seed, and delight yourself with an italian combo from Wagon Wheels Deli.

Walk or bike along the Walkway over the Hudson

The Walkway over the Hudson is the longest elevated pedestrian bridge.
